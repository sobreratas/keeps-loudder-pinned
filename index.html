FROM TRADITION TO TREND

The name KEEPS.LOUDDER has two meanings. Firstly, it is our commitment to preserving the authenticity of Lao culture, which is represented by "LOUDDER", phonetically resembling "Lao Der" (ລາອ-ເດີ້), emphasizing our connection to Lao heritage. Secondly, it reflects our goal to increase the brand's recognition, making it "louder" and more well-known.

LEARN MORE

KEEPS.LOUDDER recognizes the importance of creating eco-friendly products while incorporating ancient Lao tradition into modern designs. We are also committed to social responsiblity by sourcing materials and labor from Lao crafts people, promoting the local economy. Our long-term plan has been to improve product quality and skill through innovation, allowing us to compete more effectively in the international market and become a sustainable brand.

Our handbags can be purchased at the KEEPS.LOUDDER Studio, Vientiane Center, and other places.

We use organic cotton yarn that is spun, dyed with natural dyes, and woven on a traditional loom. These processes are all done by hand and our materials are sourced from local communities here in Laos.
